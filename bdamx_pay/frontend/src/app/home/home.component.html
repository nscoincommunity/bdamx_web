Getting Started with BDAM Pay

  • Step 1

    Integrate our easy to implement API into your online store. Set the price for your products and choose the currency you want to accept e.g. Bitcoin and Eth.

  • Step 2

    BDAM Pay is integrated with BDAM Exchange, helping us calculate real-time prices and allowing direct crypto conversion.

  • Step 3

    The customer selects the product and pays with his preferred currency e.g., USD. The amount is directly converted into Bitcoin.

  • Step 4

    You receive the amount in your wallet.
